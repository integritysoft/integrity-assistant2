What is Integrity?

Integrity is an AI-powered assistant that understands your digital activity across devices. It creates a searchable, interactive log of your digital life that you can query using natural language - helping you find what you're looking for, understand your habits, and boost your productivity.

AI-Powered Understanding

Advanced AI technology captures and understands the context of your digital activities, creating meaningful summaries and making everything searchable.

Privacy-First Design

Your data is encrypted and processed locally. Only anonymized, essential information is synced to our secure servers for AI processing.

Natural Conversation

Ask questions in everyday language: "What was I working on last Tuesday?" or "Find that spreadsheet I was editing yesterday."

Productivity Insights

Analyze your digital habits, identify productivity patterns, and receive personalized recommendations to optimize your workflow.

Customizable Settings

Control what Integrity monitors with detailed privacy settings. Exclude sensitive applications, customize monitoring schedules, and more.

Cross-Device Syncing

Seamlessly sync your activity across multiple devices, creating a unified timeline of your digital life across your entire ecosystem.

Security & Privacy

Your data security and privacy are our top priorities. Here's how we protect your information:

Local Processing

Primary data processing happens directly on your device. Only anonymized, essential data is sent to our secure servers for AI analysis.

End-to-End Encryption

All data is secured with AES-256 encryption both at rest and in transit, protecting it from unauthorized access.

Customizable Privacy

You control what Integrity can and cannot monitor. Easily exclude sensitive applications, websites, or time periods from being recorded.

Data Retention Control

Set custom data retention periods. Automatically delete data older than a specified time frame, or manually delete any records at any time.

Secure Authentication

Multi-factor authentication ensures only you can access your Integrity account and data.

Selective Monitoring

Activate "privacy mode" for sensitive activities. One-click pause for all monitoring when needed.
